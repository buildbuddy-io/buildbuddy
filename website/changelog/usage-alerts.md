You can now configure usage alerts for your BuildBuddy organization.

To access alerting, navigate to **Usage > Alerting** in the BuildBuddy UI. Daily, weekly, and monthly thresholds can be configured for any usage metric, and organization admins will receive email alerts when configured thresholds are exceeded.
